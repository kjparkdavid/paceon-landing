Questions.

How is Paceon different from Runna?
Paceon plans adapt weekly based on your actual runs — if you miss workouts, run slower than target, or skipped a day, your plan reshapes automatically. Runna plans are static. We're also about half the price with no credit card required to start your trial.
Does Paceon work without Strava?
Yes, but Strava makes it significantly better. Without Strava, you mark workouts complete manually. With Strava, runs auto-match to your plan and paces, effort levels, and completion rates feed directly into weekly adaptations.
What races can I train for?
5K, 10K, half marathon, and full marathon. Plans range from 4 to 20 weeks depending on your race date and starting fitness level.
What if I miss a workout?
Nothing bad happens. Paceon notices, adjusts next week's volume, and re-spaces your hard days. Miss a few in a row and the plan reduces load automatically — no plan guilt, no manual adjustment needed.
Can I use Paceon with Apple Watch or Garmin?
Garmin direct integration is coming soon. Most Garmin users sync through Strava, which works fully now.
How much does it cost?
Paceon is completely free right now while we're in early access. Paid plans will be introduced later — early users won't be surprised.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Cancel from the app or your App Store subscription settings at any time. You keep access until the end of your billing period.
Who makes Paceon?
Paceon is built by miuustudio, a small indie studio. We're runners ourselves — this is the tool we wanted and couldn't find.
